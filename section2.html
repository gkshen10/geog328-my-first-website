Characters

Elderbug

The town elder of Dirtmouth, who greets you when you enter the town.


Cornifer

A cartographer who can be found mapping the different areas of Hallownest, and singing a cheery tune while he works.


Iselda

A merchant who sells maps and tools at her shop in Dirtmouth. Also Cornifer's wife.


Hornet

A warrior wielding a needle and thread who is also the protagonist of the sequel game, Hollow Knight: Silksong.
