Hollow Knight

"The Knight", main character of Hollow Knight


Abstract

Hollow Knight is a platforming video game made by developer Team Cherry, in which you play as a small bug warrior and explore the secrets of the expansive kingdom of Hallownest. Along the way, you meet many interesting and unique insect friends and allies, and face off against many bug foes. The game also explores themes of loss, perserverance, and fate throughout the story.
